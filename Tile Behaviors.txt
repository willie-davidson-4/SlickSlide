Tile Behaviors:

* Break/Step Tile
o Can step on
o Can only step on once
* Once you step off, it breaks and turns into an empty tile
* Slide Tile
o Can step on
o Will automatically move you in the direction that you stepped onto the tile
o Will stop you if you hit a block tile (then you can move in any direction
* Block Tile
o Can’t step on
* Empty Tile
o Can step on
o Will kill you
* Start tile
o Your starting position that you initially have to move off of
* End Tile
o Your end position
o Can only move onto if you have stepped on ALL break/step tiles
